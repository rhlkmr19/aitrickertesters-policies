Introduction & Developer Information

📋 Developer Details

App Name: AiTricker Testers

Developer: AiTricker

Contact Email: aitrickertesters@gmail.com

Jurisdiction: Based in India, available to users worldwide

Platform: Android (Google Play Store)

AiTricker Testers is a mutual testing community platform that connects Android app developers with real testers. Developers list their apps for closed testing, and other community members test those apps in exchange for Sparks (virtual credits). The platform facilitates testing through Quick Listings and structured Pack sessions.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the AiTricker Testers mobile application, regardless of location or device. It covers all features of the App including account creation, app listing, Pack participation, Quick List testing, Sparks economy, and in-app purchases.

Information We Collect

We collect personal and non-personal information from you to provide, maintain, and improve our Services. Below is a detailed breakdown of all data we collect:

👤 Account Information (via Google Sign-In)

Email Address: Collected automatically via Google Sign-In when you create your account. This serves as your unique identifier and is used for authentication, communication, and account recovery.

Display Name: Your name as set in your Google account. Displayed to other community members in Packs, Quick Lists, and leaderboards.

Profile Photo: Your Google profile picture. Displayed alongside your name in the community.

Google User ID: A unique identifier provided by Google for authentication purposes.

Note: We do NOT store your Google password. We only receive and store OAuth authentication tokens provided by Google Sign-In.

📱 Developer App Details

If you list an app for testing on our platform, we collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • App Name — The name of your application
  • Package Name — The unique Android package identifier (e.g., com.example.myapp)
  • Play Store Link — URL to your app's Google Play Store listing
  • Closed Testing Link — The opt-in URL for your app's closed testing track
  • App Icon (Photo) — You can upload an app icon image directly from your device's photo gallery. This image is stored on Firebase Storage and displayed to other users. The image is resized to 128×128 pixels before upload.
  • App Description — A brief description of your app you provide

This information is visible to other users of the App for the purpose of facilitating testing.

📊 Usage & Activity Data

We collect data about your participation and activity within the App to operate platform features and enforce accountability:

  • Pack Activity: Which Packs you join, daily check-in status, completion or desertion status, timestamps
  • Quick List Activity: Apps you test, testing timestamps, daily testing count
  • Sparks Transactions: Sparks earned (from testing), Sparks spent (on listings), transaction history with timestamps
  • Trust Score: Your current Trust Score value and history of changes (increases and decreases)
  • Warnings & Cooldowns: Any warnings issued, cooldown periods applied, and their reasons
  • Queue Status: Your position in Quick Queue and Pack queues

💳 Payment & Transaction Information

Payment Method: We use Google Play Billing exclusively for processing all in-app purchases (Basic ₹399 and Premium ₹599 per-app plans). Google Play Billing handles all payment processing.

What We Store: We only store the following minimal transaction data:

  • Transaction amount (₹399 or ₹599)
  • Plan type purchased (Basic or Premium)
  • The app for which the plan was purchased
  • User ID of the purchaser
  • Date and time of purchase
  • Google Play order ID (for verification and support purposes)

What We Do NOT Store: We do NOT store, access, or process credit card numbers, debit card numbers, bank account details, UPI IDs, or any other payment method information. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play Billing.

🔔 Push Notification Data

We request permission to send push notifications to your device via Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM). If granted, we store:

  • FCM Token: A device-specific token used to deliver push notifications to your device
  • Notification Preferences: Your settings for which types of notifications you want to receive

Notifications are used for: Pack updates and reminders, testing reminders, Spark transaction alerts, important service announcements, and community updates.

📱 Installed Apps Data

Our App checks which apps are installed on your device to verify testing participation. Specifically:

  • Purpose: When you participate in Pack testing or Quick List testing, the App queries your device to confirm whether you have installed the required test apps
  • Permission Used: QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES (Android 11+) — allows the App to detect installed packages
  • What We Store: We store a record of which apps you have successfully tested (app package name and timestamp) in our database
  • What We Do NOT Do: We do NOT collect a full list of all apps installed on your device. We only check for specific apps that are part of your active testing assignments

This data is essential for the core functionality of the App — verifying that testers have actually installed and tested the assigned apps.

📱 Device Information

Through Firebase services, the following device information may be automatically collected:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Android OS version
  • App version installed
  • Language and locale settings
  • Time zone
  • Network type (WiFi/Mobile data — not IP address for user identification)

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, to fulfill legal obligations, or to enforce our agreements:

  • Account Data (name, email, profile photo): Retained for as long as your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.

Once we no longer need your data for the stated purposes, we will delete or permanently anonymize it.

Account Deletion

We provide you with the ability to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data.

🗑️ How to Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion through any of the following methods:

  • In-App: Navigate to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Follow the confirmation prompts to permanently delete your account.
  • Via Email: Send a deletion request to aitrickertesters@gmail.com from your registered email address with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

⚠️ What Happens When You Delete Your Account

Upon successful account deletion:

  • All your personal data (name, email, profile photo) is permanently deleted
  • All your Sparks balance is forfeited and cannot be recovered
  • All your app listings are removed from the platform
  • Your activity history, Trust Score, and Pack records are deleted
  • Your FCM notification token is invalidated
  • Any active Pack participation is terminated

Timeline: Account deletion is completed within 30 days of your request. Some data may be retained for legal obligations (transaction records for up to 3 years).

Irreversible: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You would need to create a new account to use the App again.

📌 Data Retained After Deletion

In accordance with legal requirements, the following data may be retained even after account deletion:

  • Transaction records: Purchase history (amount, date, plan type) retained for up to 3 years for legal/accounting purposes
  • Anonymized analytics: Already-aggregated anonymous usage data that cannot identify you
  • Legal compliance data: Any data required to be retained by applicable laws or regulations

Security Measures

We take commercially reasonable and industry-standard steps to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

🔐 Technical Safeguards

  • Authentication: Google Sign-In with OAuth 2.0 — we never store or handle passwords
  • Database Security: Firebase Firestore with strict security rules allowing only authenticated access
  • Server-Side Logic: Sensitive operations processed via Cloud Functions (not client-side)
  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Payment Security: Google Play Billing handles all payment data — we never see or store card details
  • Access Control: Role-based access to backend systems, limited to authorized personnel only

⚠️ Limitations

While we strive to protect your data using commercially reasonable measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by applicable law (within 72 hours for GDPR-covered users).
