Refund Conditions

A refund may be applicable in the following cases:

✅ Eligible for Refund

  • Service Not Delivered: Your app was not listed in the Pack or Quick List within 24 hours of purchase due to a platform error or technical issue on our end.
  • Technical Failure: A technical issue on our end prevented your paid plan benefits from being applied to your app (e.g., daily cap not increased, priority not applied).
  • Duplicate Purchase: You were accidentally charged twice for the same app and same plan.
  • Unauthorized Transaction: The purchase was made without your authorization (unauthorized access to your account or device).
  • Wrong Plan Purchased: You accidentally purchased the wrong plan and contact us within 24 hours before any benefits are consumed.

❌ Not Eligible for Refund

  • Change of Mind: You changed your mind after purchasing a plan.
  • App Store Rejection: Your app was rejected by Google Play (we do not guarantee app store approvals or testing outcomes).
  • Expected Testers: You did not receive the number of testers you expected (we facilitate connections, not guaranteed outcomes).
  • Policy Violations: You were removed from a Pack or had your listing removed due to violations of our Terms & Conditions.
  • Consumed Services: Your app has already been listed, tested by community members, or received any benefit from the paid plan.
  • Account Termination: Your account was terminated due to violations of our policies.
  • Third-Party Issues: Issues caused by your device, internet connection, Google account, or other factors outside our control.

Non-Refundable Items

💰 Sparks (Virtual Credits) — Non-Refundable

Sparks are a virtual, in-app credit system with absolutely no real-world monetary value. Sparks:

  • Are earned through testing activity (not purchased with real money)
  • Cannot be refunded, withdrawn, exchanged, or converted to real money
  • Cannot be sold, traded, or transferred between users or accounts
  • Are forfeited permanently upon account deletion or termination

This Refund Policy applies only to paid plans (Basic ₹399 and Premium ₹599), NOT to Sparks.

⚠️ Consumed Services

If your app has already received ANY benefit from the paid plan — including being listed in the queue, being tested by even one community member, or having the daily cap increase applied — the service is considered consumed and is not eligible for a monetary refund.

Queue Cancellation & Sparks

If you cancel your app from the Quick List before testing begins:

🔄 Pack Queue Cancellation

How to Request a Refund

📱 Option 1: Via Google Play (Within 48 Hours)

If your purchase was made less than 48 hours ago, you can request a refund directly through Google Play:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app on your device
  2. Tap your profile icon (top right)
  3. Go to Payments & subscriptions
  4. Tap Budget & order history
  5. Find the AiTricker Testers purchase
  6. Tap Request a refund or Report a problem
  7. Follow the on-screen instructions

Note: Google may approve or deny the refund at their discretion within this 48-hour window.

📧 Option 2: Via Email (After 48 Hours or Complex Issues)

If more than 48 hours have passed, or if your issue requires our direct involvement, contact us at aitrickertesters@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Your registered email address (Google account used in the App)
  • The app name and package name for which the plan was purchased
  • Plan purchased (Basic ₹399 or Premium ₹599)
  • Google Play order number (found in your purchase confirmation email or Play Store order history)
  • Date of purchase
  • Detailed reason for the refund request
  • Any supporting screenshots or evidence (if applicable)

Email Subject Line: "Refund Request — [Your App Name]"

⏱️ Timeframe for Requests

Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of the original purchase date. Requests made after 14 days will generally not be considered, except in cases of unauthorized transactions (which must be reported within 120 days per Google Play's policy).

If your refund is approved:

📌 Refund Amount

We do not offer partial refunds. All approved refunds are for the full purchase amount of the plan (₹399 for Basic or ₹599 for Premium). Google Play's service fees may apply as per Google's policies.
