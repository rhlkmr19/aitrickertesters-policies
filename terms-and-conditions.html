About AiTricker Testers

📱 What We Do

AiTricker Testers is a mutual testing community platform for Android app developers. The App facilitates a community where developers help each other by testing each other's apps through Google Play's closed testing program.

How It Works:

  • Developers list their apps for testing on our platform
  • Community members test listed apps through closed testing links
  • Testers earn Sparks (virtual credits) for completing tests
  • Sparks can be used to list your own apps for testing
  • Testing is organized through Quick Lists (individual) and Packs (group sessions)

⚠️ What We Do NOT Guarantee

We are a platform that facilitates connections between developers and testers. We do NOT guarantee:

  • A specific number of testers for your app
  • Google Play Store approval of your app
  • Positive reviews or ratings
  • Any specific outcome from the testing process

Sparks (Virtual Credits)

💰 Important: Sparks Have NO Real-World Monetary Value

Sparks are a virtual, in-app credit system used exclusively within AiTricker Testers. Sparks:

  • Have absolutely no real-world monetary value
  • Cannot be converted to real money, cryptocurrency, or any other currency
  • Cannot be sold, traded, or transferred to other users
  • Cannot be withdrawn, cashed out, or redeemed for cash
  • Cannot be transferred between accounts
  • Are non-refundable (they are earned, not purchased)

📈 Earning Sparks

  • Quick List Testing: Earn 20 Sparks per app tested (Free: 20, Basic: 25, Premium: 30)
  • Pack Completion: Earn 100 Sparks bonus upon successfully completing a 14-day Pack (free users only — paid plan users do not earn Pack completion bonus)
  • Welcome Bonus: New users receive an initial Sparks balance upon account creation

💸 Spending Sparks

  • Quick List Listing: Costs 50 Sparks to list your app in the Quick List (7 days, up to 12 testers)
  • Pack Listing: Costs 80 Sparks to join a Pack with your app

Note: Paid plan users (Basic/Premium) do NOT spend Sparks for listings — their plans cover listing fees.

⚠️ Sparks Forfeiture

We reserve the right to deduct, reset, or forfeit Sparks from your account if:

  • You violate these Terms of Service
  • You engage in fraudulent activity or Sparks manipulation
  • Your account is terminated for policy violations

All Sparks are permanently forfeited upon account deletion.

Pack Participation

Packs are structured 14-day group testing sessions:

📦 Pack Rules

  • Duration: Each Pack runs for 14 consecutive days
  • Cost: 80 Sparks to join (free users) or free with paid plans
  • Daily Requirement: You must complete a daily check-in by testing at least one app from your Pack every day
  • Completion Reward: 100 Sparks + Trust Score +20 (free users); Trust Score +20 (paid users)

🚫 Pack Desertion

If you leave or are removed from an active Pack (after it has started):

  • Your 80 Sparks investment is NOT refunded
  • Your Trust Score is reduced by 10 points
  • A 24-hour cooldown is applied before you can join another Pack

✅ Queue Cancellation (Before Start)

If you cancel your spot from a Pack queue before the Pack starts:

  • Your 80 Sparks are fully refunded
  • No penalty is applied
  • No Trust Score reduction
